Adherence Tips

A few simple habits improve consistency

Link medicines to routines

Tie reminders to breakfast, lunch, bedtime, or another everyday event that rarely changes.

Keep notes practical

Use reminder notes like "after food", "with water", or "avoid skipping" so the alert stays meaningful.

When to Update

Refresh reminders any time treatment changes

After a new prescription

Add or remove reminders when a doctor changes dosage, timing, or duration.

After side effects or confusion

If instructions are unclear, re-check the plan with your doctor or pharmacist before continuing.
