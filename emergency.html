Chest pain or collapse

Treat sudden chest pain, fainting, collapse, or severe breathing trouble as urgent and seek help immediately.

Severe bleeding or injury

Uncontrolled bleeding, major trauma, or confusion after an accident should be escalated without delay.

High fever with weakness

If fever is paired with faintness, breathing trouble, or poor responsiveness, move to urgent care quickly.
